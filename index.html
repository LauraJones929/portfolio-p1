About

Offering a multitude of acts and services, Jess Hynes is a Professional Singer from the North East of England, specialising in Weddings & large scale Private Events. Having been described as a “vocal powerhouse”, Jess has 6 years of experience performing and was the official Support Act of ‘Steps’ and ‘The Vengaboys’ at Northern Pride in 2018.

Accompanied with an astounding vocal range, her extensive repertiore is versatile enough to cater to any style of event. With sets including acoustic love songs, pop and soul grooves, folk and country hits and modern house/Ibiza style high energy dance anthems. Based in Newcastle Upon Tyne, Jess is available for performances all over the UK and internationally.
